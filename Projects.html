iOS Declarative Device Management

The iOS Declarative Device Management (DDM) project for SOTI MobiControl introduces a cutting-edge protocol for managing Apple devices running iOS 16 or later. With DDM, devices can autonomously report their status, thereby reducing network traffic and latency. This project includes enabling DDM for both new and already enrolled devices, updating the Device Details page to display DDM-related information, and supporting DDM payloads for streamlined management. The proactive approach of DDM ensures devices remain compliant with organizational policies even when offline, enhancing overall security and compliance while optimizing network efficiency.

Manage Apple TV with MobiControl

The Manage Apple TV with MobiControl project introduces a comprehensive suite of features for administrators to efficiently manage Apple TV devices. Utilizing Automated Device Enrollment (ADE), administrators can effortlessly enroll Apple TV devices into the MobiControl system, enabling rapid deployment and configuration. The project includes payloads like Feature Control, Single App Mode, and Conference Room Display Mode, allowing for tailored device setups to meet specific business needs. This initiative enhances device configuration capabilities and improves management efficiency by centralizing control within the MobiControl platform.
