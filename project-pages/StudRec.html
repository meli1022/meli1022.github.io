Student Record Keeping Database

The Shared iPad for Business feature allows multiple employees to share an iPad while providing a personalized experience for each user.The absence of Shared iPad for Business capabilities for many businesses resulted in decreased productivity, increased IT management overhead, and data security risks. With Shared iPads, multiple employees in an organization can sign in or out of a single iPad with their unique Managed Apple IDs and have app and data separation for privacy and security.
